ZENO

Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 2026

1. Data we collect

ZENO stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your name and profile photo (optional)
  • Daily energy check-ins and scores
  • Tasks, habits, and quick logs
  • App preferences (language, theme, accent color)
  • Chat history with the AI companion

2. AI features

When you use AI features, your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. We do not store these conversations on our servers. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy.

3. Cloud sync (optional)

If you enable Supabase sync, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal Supabase project. We do not have access to this data.

4. We do NOT

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your mental health information with anyone
  • Collect analytics without your consent

5. Mental health disclaimer

ZENO is a support companion app and does not replace professional medical, psychological, or therapeutic care. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a mental health professional.

6. Children

ZENO is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

7. Contact

Questions? Email us at: privacy@zeno.app
